About WAW Events

From dream to reality, celebrate with WAW.

WAW Events is a New Jersey-based décor studio bringing together elegant design, cultural sensitivity and meticulous execution. Whether it’s a wedding, engagement, baby shower, birthday or corporate event, we treat every celebration like it’s our own.

Our style is a blend of elegant, minimal, luxury, colorful, traditional and modern décor. We listen carefully, understand your vision and translate it into a cohesive décor story — from the main stage to the smallest corner.

We currently style events across New Jersey, New York and Pennsylvania, partnering closely with venues and families to ensure décor is on time, on-theme and effortless for you.

What you can expect with WAW Events:

  • Personal consultation to understand your story & preferences
  • Customized moodboards & décor ideas for your event
  • Clear communication on budgets, options and timelines
  • Dedicated on-site team on your event day
